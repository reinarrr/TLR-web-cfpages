Our Beliefs.

Established on the Bible, this is our foundational statement of faith.

Bible-based • Christ-centered • Missional

One God

We believe in one omnipotent, omniscient, and omnipresent God who created and sustains everything. God exists in three persons: the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit.

Matthew 28:19; 2 Corinthians 13:14

The Lord Jesus Christ

Jesus Christ is the eternal Son of God, born of a virgin, embodying both divine and human nature. He died, was resurrected on the third day, ascended into heaven, and will return with great power and glory.

Luke 1:26-35; John 1:18; Isaiah 7:14; Isaiah 9:6

The Holy Scriptures

The Old and New Testaments of the Bible are divinely inspired and infallible, forming the authoritative foundation for all matters of faith and conduct.

2 Timothy 3:16; 1 Corinthians 2:13

Justification By Faith

Salvation is a free gift from God, obtained through repentance, forgiveness of sins, and faith in Jesus Christ’s death and resurrection.

Ephesians 2:8-9; Hebrews 9:12; Romans 5:11

The Enemy

The Devil instigated humanity’s fall and continues his work to destroy mankind. Ultimately, he will face eternal judgment and be cast into the Lake of Fire.

Matthew 25:41; Revelation 20:14-15

Baptism In Water

Christ’s followers are commanded to be baptized in the name of the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit through immersion in water.

Matthew 28:19; Acts 10:44-48

Baptism In The Holy Spirit

A genuine experience that occurs at or after salvation, marked by speaking in tongues as the Spirit enables.

Acts 2:1-4

Communion

Communion is a time of fellowship among believers and with God, obeying Christ’s command to remember His sacrifice on the Cross.

1 Corinthians 11:28-32; Matthew 26:26-28

Gifts of the Holy Spirit

The Holy Spirit bestows gifts evident in the early Church, intended to build up the congregation.

1 Corinthians 12-14

Eternal Life

Believers will receive eternal life, while those who do not believe face eternal damnation.

John 5:24; John 3:16; Revelation 20:10-15

The Church

The universal Church consists of all local congregations around the world whose members acknowledge Jesus as Lord and Savior.

Matthew 16:18; Acts 15:22

The Return of Christ

Jesus Christ will physically return to earth in the same manner he ascended, with great power and authority, to reign as King and judge all humanity.

John 14:2-3; 1 Thessalonians 4:13-18
