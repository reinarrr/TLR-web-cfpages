Now Think About This

The Spirit then drove Jesus to go into the wilderness. (Mark 1.12)

We never really think of the Holy Spirit needing to work to move Jesus, even to the point of compulsion. But there it is. Also, we don't think of Jesus as not on the move. But here we see that he was compelled to go... into the wilderness. Well, I suppose I see the hesitation. Who would want to go be lost in a dangerous place?

We find, of course, there is nothing amiss with the Holy Spirit's leading. Even if it feels like you're lost.
