The God of the living.

"They were amazed by His answer, and they became silent."

Many people asked Jesus questions. Jesus always answered truthfully, directly and powerfully. Whenever He answered, people recognised the authority and validity of His message.

Today, we have access to the Master’s words and answers to life’s questions. They are available in multiple languages and variety of versions. His words and their potency have not changed. What we do need to do more is be silent. We need to hear His message and recognise His authority.

God of the Living

So he is the God of the living, not the dead, for they are all alive to him. (Luke 20.38)

This sentence is worthy of a sermon series. Just know this reassurance: as Jesus is explaining life after death through tracing history, He is also drawing our future in this answer. We are all alive in Him.

Standing Firm

By standing firm, you will win your souls. (Luke 21.19)

It may not win popularity. It may not get us the most friends. It may not bring the accolades we seek. We might even find ourselves hated (Luke 21.17). But past all that, it is faithfulness that brings life.

Caught Unaware

Don’t let that day catch you unaware. (Luke 21.34)

Distraction is the biggest reason people are caught unaware. That is, we know what to look out for. We know the signs. But our attention is pulled elsewhere. Distracted.

We’re told not to use our phones while we are driving. Because we will be distracted. And accidents happen when we are caught unaware. Are you distracted? Okay, how about this: where is your focus set today?

The Challenge

Big Steps Forward

01.
Respect and Honour

Actively find ways to honour Jesus in your actions and thoughts today, perhaps by starting your day with a dedicated prayer or showing intentional kindness to others.

02.
Affirm Eternal Life

God is the God of the living. Write a personal affirmation of faith focused on the eternal life Jesus promises and refer to it during moments of doubt or fear.

03.
Mindful Focus

Disconnect from the world's noise at a set time today. Focus solely on God's word and presence to reduce distractions and stay spiritually aligned.
