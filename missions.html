What is Open Doors?

At The Living Room, Open Doors represents the opportunities God has placed before us to advance His Kingdom. It is a call to step into missions with courage, faith, and obedience.

We respond to God’s invitation to bring the Gospel to the nations through digital evangelism, supporting global workers, and engaging in local outreach.

Your Mission, Should You Choose to Accept It...

From the very beginning, the Gospel has been about movement—Jesus walking from village to village, His disciples crossing borders to bring hope.
