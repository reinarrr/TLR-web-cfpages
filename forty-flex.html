What Is 40Flex?

40Flex is a 40-day journey through the four Gospels, leading up to Resurrection Sunday. For over eight years, we’ve been walking through the life of Jesus together, discovering fresh revelations year after year.

The disciples walked physically with Jesus; today, we walk with Him through His Word, prayer, and obedience. This is more than a devotional—it’s an invitation to stay close to Him and walk at His pace.

"As we walk with Jesus, we walk together. No one gets left behind. We are here to build good habits that last a lifetime."

Join the Journey: Start reading, follow along every day, and invite someone to walk with you.

FAQ

Can I start late?

Yes! Just jump in where we are or start from Day 01 at your own pace.

Do I need a partner?

You can walk alone, but inviting someone to join is highly encouraged for shared growth.

What do I need?

Simply a Bible, a journal, and an open heart to what the Word reveals.
